Summary: Latitude vs. Windspeed

This graph compares Latitude vs Windspeed. It appears that in the Southern Hemisphere there are much more cities with lower windspeeds closer to the equator. If low wind speeds and warmer temperatures are part of ideal vacation, you may want to consider a vacation in the Southern Hemisphere.
